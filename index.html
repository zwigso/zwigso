What is zwigso and how can it help my restaurant?

zwigso is a smart burn control and optimization platform designed for restaurants. We help you to detect hidden charges, track promo usage, and uncover platform inefficiencies boosting your profit margins with actionable insights.

How does zwigso detect hidden charges and burn?

zwigso tracks real time data from your delivery platforms and analyzes payment structures, promo deductions, and revenue splits using intelligent algorithms. This helps spot unnecessary charges or unauthorized discounts quickly.

Will zwigso disrupt my current operations?

zwigso runs seamlessly alongside your existing delivery workflows. It’s a plug and monitor solution, there’s no interference with your day to day operations, no downtime, and no need to change how you already work. You’ll simply gain better visibility, control, and insights without any disruptions.

Yes. Our expert support team is always available to answer your queries and resolve the issues you are facing.
